 Email Index and Uniqueness

1. Schema Changes:
   File: models/User.js
   Change: Added `unique: true, index: true` to the email field.
   Reason: Prevents duplicate user emails and makes email searches faster.

2. Validation Test:
   Tool: Postman
   Test 1: POST /users with new email -> Result: 201 Created
   Test 2: POST /users with same email -> Result: 500 Error, E11000 duplicate key error
   Conclusion: Uniqueness constraint is working correctly.
